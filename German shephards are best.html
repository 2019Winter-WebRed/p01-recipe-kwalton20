All about German shephards and why they are the best dogs

German shephards are a medium-large sized working dog that originate from Germany. They are very loyal dogs and love to protect their owners but at the same time they are great around kids too. German Shepards are the second most popular dog breed according to registrations.They are also very intelligent

Some more specifics about german shephards are that the average height (male) is 24-26in for female it is 22-24 in. The average weight (male) is around 65-90 pounds and for female it is 50-70 pounds. Germna shephards are confident, smart and courageous. They are a herding dog so they love to chase things.
