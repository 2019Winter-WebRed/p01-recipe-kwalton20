How to make Mac n Cheese

Ingrediants

  • chedder cheese
  • elbow noodles
  • milk
  • seasoning of your choice

Directions

  1. Boil your water in a pot
  2. Add in your elbow pasta after the water is done boiling
  3. Stir till the pasta is squishy
  4. While your'e waiting for your Mac n Cheese to boil start making your cheese
  5. melt chedder cheese in bowl and add in milk once melted
  6. Add in your pasta to your melted cheese
  7. Stir till mixed
  8. Finally, serve in a bowl and enjoy!
